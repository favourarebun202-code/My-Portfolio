About Me

About Me

Fashion Designer

I’m a professional fashion designer and the founder of Adonai Fashion Home in Ogun State. I am passionate about creating unique, high-quality garments for everyone—men, women, and children alike. My expertise ranges from crafting modern English wears to recreating complex Nigerian cultural designs with a contemporary twist.

Beyond tailoring and garment amendments, I am a trusted provider of premium sewing accessories and a dedicated educator. Through our fashion school and apprenticeship programs, I am committed to training the next generation of designers in the art of creative tailoring and advanced fashion recreation. I approach every project with warmth and a positive attitude, ensuring every client feels welcome at Adonai Fashion Home.
